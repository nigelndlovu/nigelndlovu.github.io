Site Name

The site name will be PSFweather.com. The name suggests that the site will give weather information for three towns abbreviated by PSF.

Site Purpose

The main purpose of the site is to give a report on the daily weather patterns of the three towns, Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The site will also give warnings of severe weather conditions that may affect the three towns and surrounding areas. It will also give a news report of events in relation to weather conditions or promotion happening around the three towns and surrounding areas.
